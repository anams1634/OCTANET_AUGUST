About us

Ann's Bakery is a seasonal cafe and marketplace featuring fresh baked goods in East Hollywood, CA run by Pastry Chef/Baker Roxana Jullapat and Chef Daniel Mattern. Longtime champions of produce-driven cooking, Jullapat and Mattern built their careers over fifteen years in renowned kitchens of Los Angeles (including AOC, Lucques, Campanile, and Cooks County) before opening of Ann's. At Ann's, they continue their dedication to hand made food with artisan breads and pastries made daily with locally-lysourced, heirloom grains, as well as a produce forward day-time menu
